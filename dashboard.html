Student Problems & Our Smart Solution

Problem Faced by Students

Students struggle to find proper notes, PYQs, important questions and study materials from multiple platforms like WhatsApp, random websites and libraries. This wastes time and creates confusion especially during exams.

Our Solution – GoldNotes

GoldNotes provides a centralized platform where students can download organized notes, PYQs and important study material in just one click. Everything is structured year-wise and subject-wise, saving time and improving exam preparation.

Developed by Team Hackverse - Ritik Pandey (COMPUTER)
THIS PLATFORM IS ACTUALLY DEVELOPED TO HELP SLRTCE COMPUTER STUDENTS FROM 1ST YEAR TO 4TH YEAR IN TERMS OF BEST NOTES AND BEST YT LINKS.
